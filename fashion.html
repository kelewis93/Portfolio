Couture Models was created at Southern Illinois University Edwardsville in 2013. Jalen Washington and DeVonte Dixon brought the orginazation to Eastern Illinois University in 2014 after transferring schools.Jalen Washington is a junior who wants to teach people how to be comfortable in their own skin through modeling and performing on campus. Subcultures is a small group of people with the same interest or simulatiry in a large community. This organization is a subculture who enjoys displaying their own style as well as PERFORMING as different characters created by the president Jalen Washinton.
